## Open-Source GitHub Projects

### Dedicated AI Legal Tech & Open Law Firm Tools

- **[Open Legal AI](https://github.com/search?q=open+legal+ai)**  
  Community-driven open-source legal AI tools and frameworks for document analysis and automation.

- **[LawLLM / Legal AI Models](https://github.com/search?q=law+llm+open+source)**  
  Open-source legal language models and fine-tunes for contract review, research, and drafting.

- **[DocTR Legal](https://github.com/mindee/doctr)**  
  Open-source document text recognition toolkit widely used in legal tech for OCR and extraction.

- **[LangChain Legal Agents](https://github.com/langchain-ai/langchain)**  
  Frameworks for building AI legal assistants with retrieval, reasoning, and document handling.

- **[CrewAI Legal Crews](https://github.com/crewAIInc/crewAI)**  
  Multi-agent orchestration for building collaborative legal AI teams (researcher, drafter, reviewer).

- **[LlamaIndex Legal RAG](https://github.com/run-llama/llama_index)**  
  Data framework for building legal knowledge bases and retrieval systems.

- **[Harvey AI Open Alternatives](https://github.com/search?q=harvey+ai+open+source)**  
  Community reproductions of AI legal research and analysis tools.

- **[Legal Robot Open Implementations](https://github.com/search?q=legal+robot+open+source)**  
  Open-source tools for contract analysis and legal document processing.

- **[Open Source Legal Tech](https://github.com/search?q=open+source+legal+tech)**  
  Various repositories for open legal document automation and practice management.

- **[OSCAL](https://github.com/usnistgov/OSCAL)** (and related compliance tools)  
  Open-source frameworks for regulatory compliance and legal automation.

### Additional Strong Open-Source Options

- **[PDFPlumber / PyMuPDF** for legal document parsing and extraction.
- **[spaCy + Legal NER** models for named entity recognition in contracts.
- **[Hugging Face Legal Models** — Various open legal LLMs and fine-tunes.
- **[n8n Legal Workflows** for automated document processing and compliance checks.
- **[Dify Legal Agents** for building custom legal AI applications.

**Frameworks for building custom legal AI**: Combine **LangChain**, **LlamaIndex**, **CrewAI**, and **DocTR** with **Ollama** to create fully open and sovereign legal AI systems.
